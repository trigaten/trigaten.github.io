Teacher Recommender System (TRS)

Total lines written: 0

Code lines written: 0

keywords
Google Apps Script (GAS), Scriplets, Asynchronous client-side JavaScript, Callbacks

What?

TRS is an end-to-end data collection and analysis system which allows college counselors at high schools to collect preference data from students and teachers on who they want to write their college recommendation letter and who they want to write for respectively.

Why?

The current system of assigning students to teacher recommenders often consists of students finding teachers, asking them to be their recommender, then the student reporting this to the college counselors. This system leads to a number of issues including, but not limited to: teachers getting overwhelmed with students for whom to write, students taking weeks to locate their teachers and report to their counselors, suboptimal groupings being formed where teachers might feel that they must write for students with whom they don't have much experience, and finally general disorganization.

How?

After considering a number of platforms to build on, I decided that the Google Workspace development environment was optimal for this project. I didn't have to worry about security, sign in, or servers. Additionally, most users would be using a platform which was already familiar to them and installing the add-on is easy.

I built a Google Sheet Add-On with a Sidebar menu which provides buttons to execute different functionality. After certain steps, reports are generated as Google Sheets.
